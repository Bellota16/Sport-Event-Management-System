Football, also known as soccer, is a globally popular sport played by two teams of eleven players each. The objective is to score goals by kicking a ball into the opponent's net. It requires skill, strategy, teamwork, and physical prowess. The game is governed by rules and regulations, ensuring fair play. Football unites fans worldwide, sparking passion, rivalries, and unforgettable moments on the pitch. The sport's history, legendary players, and iconic tournaments like the FIFA World Cup have shaped its cultural significance.
